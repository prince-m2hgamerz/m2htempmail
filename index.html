How Does Disposable Email Work?

A temporary or "throwaway" email is a short-lived address that is generated automatically. Unlike traditional providers like Gmail or Outlook, M2H Mail requires no registration. Our system creates a unique mailbox in our secure RAM cache that listens for incoming messages for a specific duration. Once you close your browser or the session expires, the mailbox and all its contents are permanently wiped from our servers.
